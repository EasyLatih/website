Understanding HRD Corp Claimable Courses

HRD Corp Claimable Courses is a strategic initiative by the Human Resource Development Corporation (HRD Corp) to support Malaysian employers in enhancing their workforce's skills. These courses are eligible for financial claims by registered employers under the Pembangunan Sumber Manusia Berhad (PSMB) Act 2001.

What Are HRD Corp Claimable Courses?

HRD Corp Claimable Courses are training programmes that employers can send their employees to, either through public sessions or in-house arrangements. These courses must meet certain criteria to qualify for funding, including:

  • A minimum duration of four (4) hours
  • Delivery by a registered HRD Corp Training Provider
  • Courses aligned with business or operational needs
