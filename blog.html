Understanding HRD Corp Claimable Courses

Easy Latih Consultancy is a registered HRD Corp Training Provider offering fully claimable programmes for Malaysian employers under the HRD Corp Claimable Courses initiative.

What Are HRD Corp Claimable Courses?

HRD Corp Claimable Courses are training programmes that employers can send their employees to, either through public sessions or in-house arrangements. These courses must meet specific criteria to qualify for funding:

  • A minimum duration of four (4) hours
  • Delivered by a registered HRD Corp Training Provider
  • Aligned with business or operational needs
